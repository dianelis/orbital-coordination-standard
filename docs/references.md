# References

This draft is intended to align with existing space traffic coordination and navigation data work.

- CCSDS Conjunction Data Message, CCSDS 508.0-B-1
- CCSDS Orbit Data Messages, including OMM, OEM, and OCM
- NOAA Office of Space Commerce Traffic Coordination System for Space
- NASA Starling and SpaceX Starlink space traffic coordination experiment

These references are not dependencies for the toy validator. They are architectural anchors for future interoperability work.
